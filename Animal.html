Black Panther

One of the strongest of tree climbing big cats, they will often pounce on their prey from within a tree, and are capable of leaping 20 feet into the air.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera Onca(jaguar species) or panthera Pardus(leopard species)
  • Average Length: 3 - 6.4 ft.
  • Average Height: 2 - 2.5 ft.
  • Average Weight: 68 - 210 lbs.
  • Average Speed: 36 mph
  • Lifespan: 10 - 17 yrs.
  • Habitats: Rainforest, marshlands, woodlands, savannahs, mountains, and deserts.

Black Panther is the common name given any species of the Panthera family with black fur. In Asia and Africa this includes the leopard, and in the Americas the jaguar. Black Panthers are known to be very smart and stealthy hunters using their black coat to hide and stalk prey easily.
